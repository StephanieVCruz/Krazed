Descendants of the Sun premiered in 2016. The drama follows the romance between a Korean Solider and a Doctor and how different their lives are. While one's duty is to kill to protect lives while the other has taken an oath to save lives.

Goblinview_headline
closeGoblin

Goblin (also known by the title "Guardian: The Lonely and Great God"), follows an immortal goblin and protector of souls. He an the Grim Reaper both see souls to the afterlife. His life changes when he meets a girl who can see ghosts. The same girl is also destined to be his bride and turn him to ashes.

Personal Tasteview_headline
closePersonal Taste

Personal Taste follows furniture designer Park Kae-In, who is disorganized and a bit of a slob, living alone in a hanok (traditional Korean house) built by her father. She finds that her old roommate and ex-boyfriend have been cheating on her for some time, with the two to be wed. Having an extra space for a roommate, she allows Jeon Jin-ho, an architect, to live with her under the false assumption he is gay. Romance and hijinks ensue in the kdrama romance.

Soulmateview_headline
closeSoulmate

Soulmate follows 6 different people with fate having other things in mind for their romantic lives. It has the ongoing themes of fate, finding your other half, and how life may not always go according to plan.

W: Two Worldsview_headline
closeW: Two Worlds

Oh Yeon-joo is a cardiothoracic surgeon who life turns upside when her webcomic artist father goes missing. In hopes of searching for him, she is transported in her father's best selling webcomic, W. She meets the main character of the webcomic and begins a chain reaction with her actions becoming a part of the webcomic itself.

Weightlifting Fairyview_headline
closeWeightlifting Fairy

Weightlifing Fairy Kim Bok-Joo is a coming of age sports drama, inspired by the life of Olympic gold-medalist Jang Mi-ran. It follows its main characters having themes of preserverance being an athlete and romance as well.
