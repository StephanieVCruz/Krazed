closeGirls' Generation

Making their debut in 2007, Girls' Generation is a nine-peice girl group having popularity in South Korea, China, and Japan. Their skyrocket to fame was started by their hit single "Gee" and they proceeded to have hits to keep them at the top of the music charts such as "Tell Me Your Wish" and "Run Devil Run". They continue to be one of South Korea's most iconic kpop group to this day.

closeRed Velvet

Although very new to the Kpop scene, that fact hasn't stopped Red Velvet from making waves. Making their debut in 2014, they have already won multiple awards for Best New Artist, and 26 awards won in total. Their name combines the intent behind their music and songs.
